# FTX, political donations, and campaign finance charges

Source: https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/ftx-founder-indicted-fraud-money-laundering-and-campaign-finance-offenses
Source: https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/samuel-bankman-fried-found-guilty-seven-counts-fraud-and-conspiracy
Source: https://www.fec.gov/updates/commission-notices-political-committees-about-crypto-contributions/

The Department of Justice indictment of Sam Bankman-Fried included campaign finance
allegations along with fraud and money laundering allegations. Prosecutors alleged
that customer money was misused and that political contributions were made through
straw donors. The Federal Election Commission separately provides rules and guidance
for political committees handling cryptocurrency contributions. The campaign finance
allegations connected FTX, Sam Bankman-Fried, customer funds, political committees,
and federal election law.

Relations:

- Department of Justice -> indicted -> Sam Bankman-Fried
- indictment -> included -> campaign finance allegations
- indictment -> included -> fraud allegations
- indictment -> included -> money laundering allegations
- prosecutors -> alleged misuse of -> customer funds
- prosecutors -> alleged use of -> straw donors
- Sam Bankman-Fried -> associated with -> FTX
- FTX customer funds -> connected to -> political contributions allegation
- Federal Election Commission -> regulates -> political committees
- political committees -> may receive -> cryptocurrency contributions
