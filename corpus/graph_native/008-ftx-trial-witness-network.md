# FTX trial witness network

Source: https://www.justice.gov/usao-sdny/pr/samuel-bankman-fried-sentenced-25-years-prison
Source: https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/caroline-ellison-former-ceo-alameda-research-pleads-guilty-fraud-charges
Source: https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/former-ftx-executive-gary-wang-pleads-guilty-fraud-charges

The criminal case against Sam Bankman-Fried featured former FTX and Alameda insiders.
Caroline Ellison, former chief executive of Alameda Research, pleaded guilty to
fraud charges and cooperated with prosecutors. Gary Wang, a co-founder of FTX,
also pleaded guilty and cooperated. Nishad Singh, a former FTX engineering director,
pleaded guilty in a related case. These witnesses connected FTX's exchange business,
Alameda's trading activity, and the Department of Justice prosecution.
