# OpenAI leadership crisis and Microsoft

Source: https://openai.com/blog/openai-announces-leadership-transition
Source: https://openai.com/blog/sam-altman-returns-as-ceo-openai-has-a-new-initial-board
Source: https://blogs.microsoft.com/blog/2023/11/20/microsoft-openai-partnership/

In November 2023, OpenAI's board removed Sam Altman as chief executive. Greg
Brockman stepped down as board chair and later left his role. Mira Murati was
named interim chief executive, then Emmett Shear was named interim chief executive
during the crisis. Microsoft, OpenAI's major strategic partner, said it remained
committed to the partnership. Satya Nadella later said Microsoft would hire Sam
Altman and Greg Brockman to lead a new advanced AI research team if they did not
return to OpenAI. OpenAI later announced Sam Altman would return as chief executive
with a new initial board including Bret Taylor, Larry Summers, and Adam D'Angelo.
