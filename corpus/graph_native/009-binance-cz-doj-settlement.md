# Binance, Changpeng Zhao, and the DOJ settlement

Source: https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/binance-and-ceo-plead-guilty-federal-charges-4b-resolution
Source: https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/binance-founder-changpeng-zhao-sentenced-prison

Binance is a large cryptocurrency exchange founded by Changpeng Zhao, also known as
CZ. In 2023, Binance and Changpeng Zhao pleaded guilty to federal charges in a
resolution with the United States Department of Justice and other agencies. Binance
agreed to pay more than four billion dollars. Changpeng Zhao stepped down as chief
executive and was later sentenced to prison. Binance had also appeared in the FTX
collapse timeline because it considered and then abandoned a rescue acquisition of
FTX in November 2022.
